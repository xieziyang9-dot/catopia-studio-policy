Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This page applies to the game Catopia Studio. We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. Below are the methods you can use to request the deletion of your data associated with Catopia Studio on the Facebook Instant Games platform.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data by following any of the methods below:

Method 1: Email Request

Send an email to xieziyang9@gmail.com with the subject line "Catopia Studio - Data Deletion Request".

Please include:

  • Your Facebook display name
  • The game name: Catopia Studio
  • A brief description of your request

Method 2: Through Facebook Settings

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy → Settings.
  2. Navigate to Apps and Websites.
  3. Find Catopia Studio and click Remove.
  4. Check the box to delete all data associated with the app.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your game profile and account information
  • Game progress, scores, and achievements
  • In-game preferences and settings
  • Any other personal data associated with your account in Catopia Studio

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted.

Important Notes

  • Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. All game progress and purchases will be lost.
  • We may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
  • Deleting your data will not affect any data held directly by Facebook/Meta.
